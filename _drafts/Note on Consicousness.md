## Basic View on Consciousness

* **Level of conscious** should be separated from **content of consciousness**
  * Level of consciousness ~ arousal , loss of consciousnees -> no content

## V1 and NCC

* V1 contains visual information that is in-accessible from visual conscious. 
  * If image is shined only through one eye, the eye source of input is unreachable from consciousness. But decodable from V1 neural activity. 
* Neural activity in V1 does not correlate with some of our perceptual experience. 
  * In Chromatic flickering, V1 neuron can follow the color flickering even when perceptually it have been blended together. 
* Some perceptual changes are not reflected in V1 activity 
  * In binocular rivalry, Perceptual changes does not come from change of stimulus property. And V1 reflects the stimulus property more than the perceptual changes  (Single unit monkey experiment )
    * But in human the fluctuation and perceptual rivalry correlates (fMRI human study)
